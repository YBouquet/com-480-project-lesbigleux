Context- Which countries are represented in which film ceremonies over time

Introduction

When the Oscar ceremony become questionnable, what can we learn from Cinema? This work is an attempt to illustrate and ask multiple question about cinema, culture, tendance in general. From a serie of datasets, we manage to ask collected data about tacit rules that are often kept deeply into our mind.

What are the core visualization presented here

The Network

The network implemented here represents the country involved in the main film award events in 2019. The blue nodes represent the events, the orange one represent the countries. A contry is involved in an event if at least one film produced in this country is nominated in the event.

Interaction

You can drag any node you want. You can isolate the node of group (countries or event) next to the other group to ease the readibility of connections. For example, take a blue node, if you place it to the right of the orange nodes, it will keep its place, otherwise it will return to its original x-coordinate. The same idea goes with an orange node. A force exists between the nodes, so that they won't cover each other up.

Further Ideas

We want to bring text and legends in the visualization so that those explanation won't be necessary anymore. Also we aim to bring a timeline so that this example of 2019 can be extended to any year that offers data in our datasets. Also, we want the user to be able to click on an event to display a sequence sunburst detailing what that event was in the considered year.

The Sequence Sunburst

It represents the awards repartition (Oscar), the winners and nominees repartition, the films/people repartition, and give all the awards category repartition for the Academy Awards in 2019.

Interaction

You can put your mouse on any partition of the sequence and the latter would appear in detail above the rings.

Further Ideas

The sunburst would appear and be updated according to the interaction the user woudl have with the network as explained above : clicking on a node representing an event. It would also be linked to the timeline of the network so that an update of the partitions would be triggered according to the new targeted year.

Exploratory -Evolution of genres and awards in a specific country

Main goal

The section aims to correlate the films produced by country according to their genre and the awards received over time.
Has a country been able to change the type of film it produces in order to have a better cinematic impact?

Pic of history impact in Iraq

Observe a pic of an historical film production around 2011 -2012, only time this kind of film was produced and primed. The corresponding film is Bahman Ghobadi's 'Fasle kargadan', a big success film with 6 wins and 6 nominations in Asian Film Awards(2013), Chicago International Film Festival (2012), Tallinn Back Nights Film Festival (2012).
Marker of an intellectual revolution at work in Iraq through cinema.

Strong diversity in France

It can be noted that France produces almost all possible genres and each of these genres follow the same evolution, with the renown in agreement. More films, more renown. We can therefore deduce that in addition to being a major film producer, the films are of high quality.

Comedia in Russia

We can observe that comedy is a very widespread genre in the films produced (click on By proportion) Soviet comedy films are part of the world's film heritage. Russia, with its history marked by tragic episodes, has been able to place humour at the heart of its traditional and here Soviet film culture.

How to use

You can put your mouse on any genre or type of award to highlight the corresponding evolution.
You can zoom in each graph by dragging a selecting box to extend the desired zone.
You can choose how you want to visualize the data : by proportion or with the corresponding number of film produced.

Conclusion - Overview of countries' trends through cinema awards

After accumulating the general information with the two above visualizations, one may ask himself how did the repartition of the countries, linked with the film genres, evolve through time based on if the movie has been nominated or has won in the most prestigious film ceremonies. What is the proportion of french drama movies that have won in the 60's? How many comedy movies have been nominated these past 10 years in the USA?

How to use

You can explore the questions stated above as well as many others in the visualization presented just below. You can first start by selecting some time range you want to explore by clicking on one of the many ranges in the timeline. You can then hover on one colorful line to get more details about the proportion of movies sharing the attributes where the line goes through. One is also able to change the order of the parallel planes by clicking and moving one attribute to another place on the line it is on. This way, you are able to get many other insights that interest you.
